# Sending Invite to Cutting App

In the Backend Portal, when creating a new user, if the user’s role is a harvester or forklift operator, tick the “**Send****CuttingApp****Invitation**” checkbox. An email invitation with a link to download the app will be sent to the user. (See Figure below)
