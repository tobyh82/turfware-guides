# Viewing Orders

You can toggle between daily orders and 5-day forecasts by switching the button at the top.

#### **Daily** **Orders:**

- It lists all orders of the date. You can change the date by clicking on the forward or backward arrow icon next to the date.

- The left panel provides an overall snapshot of the orders and areas harvested.

- **Key****Metrics** **Displayed:**
  - Total Orders:Displays the total harvested orders compared to the overall orders for the period.
  - Total Area (SQM):Shows the harvested area (in square meters) compared to the total area.
- **Pallet** **Types:**
  - W Pallets: Display the harvested "Wooden" pallets compared to the total "Wooden" pallets.
  - S Pallets: Display the harvested "Steel" pallets compared to the total "Steel" pallets.
  - Meters Harvested Progress:Visual progress bar indicating the percentage of harvested meters relative to the total.
  - Meters RTD (Ready to Deliver) Progress:Progress bar showing the percentage of meters ready to deliver compared to the total area.

- The right panel contains a detailed breakdown for each grass type, allowing you to monitor the progress of individual categories.

## 5 Day Forecast:

- The 5-Day Forecast tab provides cutting orders for the upcoming 5 days.
