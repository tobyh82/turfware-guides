# Dashboards

Turfware's dashboards give you an **at-a-glance read on the business** — accessed from the **top navigation** bar. Each one answers a different question.

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   __[Planner](planner.md)__

    Forward capacity — trucks, pallets, drops and orders across the next four weeks.

-   __[Cut Sheet](cut-sheet.md)__

    What needs cutting — order and square-metre totals, and harvest progress.

-   __[Forecast](forecast.md)__

    The short-term look ahead — order, delivery and laying volumes, plus outstanding payments.

-   __[Sales](sales.md)__

    Sales performance — this year vs last, by segment, with volumes, customers and a delivery map.

</div>
