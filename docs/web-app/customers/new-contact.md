# New Contact

- Navigate Customer: On the left-hand side (LHS) navigation panel, go to Customers

- Access the Create CustomerOption: In the Customers Management page, click on the green **New Contact** button at the top right corner.

- Or can bulk import Customers by clicking on the drop-down indicator next to the “New Contact” text. Then click “**ImportContact - CSV**”, and upload the CSV file.
