# Sending Invoice - Automation

Follow these steps to enable automated invoice sending to your customers:

1. **Access Company Information**
   - Go to **System Settings** from the left sidebar menu.
   - Select **Company Information**.
2. **Enable Invoice Automation**
   - Toggle the **Sending Invoice Automation** switch to **ON**.
3. **Set the Delivery Timing**
   - Choose whether the invoice will be sent **before** or **after** the delivery date.
   - Specify the number of days (either before or after) for the invoice to be sent to the customer.
   - Select the specific time when the sending automation will start.
4. **Save Changes**
   1. Click the **Save** button to apply your changes.

*Once saved, invoices for all confirmed orders with the specified delivery date will be automatically sent based on the selected time.*

### Important Notes:

**Sending Today's Invoices**  
To send invoices for all orders with today's delivery date, set the number of days to **0**. This will ensure all invoices for today's deliveries are sent.

**Turning Off Automation**  
To disable automation, simply toggle the **Sending Invoice Automation** switch to **OFF**.

**Pre-requisites**  
Ensure that your Xero Chart of Accounts and Products are fully set up before enabling this feature.

**No Duplicate Invoices**  
Invoices that have already been sent will not be resent.
