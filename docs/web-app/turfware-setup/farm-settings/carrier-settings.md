# Carrier Settings

**Carriers** are the **delivery companies** you use to get orders to customers — your own fleet or third-party freight providers. They're assigned to delivery runs in Logistics, and to [Farm Groups](farm-groups.md).

## Where to find it

Left-hand navigation → **Farm Settings → Carrier Settings**. Click **Create** to add a carrier, or click a row to edit.

## Setting up a carrier

- **Name** — the carrier / delivery company name *(required)*.
- **Email** — a contact email for the carrier.

Save, and the carrier is available to assign to delivery runs and farm groups.
