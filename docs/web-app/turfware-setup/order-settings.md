# Order Settings

Order Settings control **what appears on the order page**, so you only ever see what your business actually offers. This keeps the order layout clean and stops staff getting confused by options you don't use.

## Where to set them up

Go to **System Settings → Company Information → Order Settings**.

## Show or hide order options

Toggle **on or off** the products and services that are available on an order page.

For example, if your business doesn't provide **services**, switch Services off — and it won't appear as an option when creating an order, keeping the layout simple. The same choices flow through to your **Farm Settings** setup.

## Installation & Delivery — accounting mapping

Under Order Settings, **Installation** and **Delivery** are also where you select the **Chart-of-Accounts (COA)** and **Item code** that link them to your accounting system (Sawfish) — so freight and installation charges post to the right accounts on the invoice.

*[Screenshot: Installation & Delivery COA / Item mapping]*

## Save

Click **Save changes**. Your order pages immediately reflect the options you've turned on, and Installation/Delivery charges map to the accounts you set.

!!! note "Ties into the order page"
    What you enable here is exactly what shows in the **Products & Services** section when [creating an order](../order-management/creating-and-managing-an-order.md).
