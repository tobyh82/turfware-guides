# Shopify Pending

**Shopify Pending Orders** is the inbound queue for orders placed through your **Shopify online store**. When a customer buys online, the order syncs into Turfware and lands here, waiting for your team to action it and bring it into the normal order workflow.

## Where to find it

Left-hand navigation → **Order Management → Shopify Pending**.
