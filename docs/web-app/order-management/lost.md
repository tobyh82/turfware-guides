# Lost

An order marked **Lost** is one that's no longer going ahead — **cancelled, abandoned, or unfulfilled**. It's effectively **closed and archived**, with no further action required.

## Where to find it

Left-hand navigation → **Order Management → Lost**.

## Why it's kept

Lost orders aren't deleted — they're a record of demand that didn't convert. Keeping them lets you **track lost business** and hold information on lost customers for **follow-up and marketing**.
