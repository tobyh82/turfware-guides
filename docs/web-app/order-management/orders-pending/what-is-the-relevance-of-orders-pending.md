# What is the relevance of orders pending?

When orders are pending, they remain outside the operational workflow, meaning they do not appear in preparation, laying, harvesting, or delivery workflows. These orders are held in this status until they are confirmed or paid by clients, ensuring that only finalised orders proceed into the operations flow.
