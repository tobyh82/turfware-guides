# What is the relevance of orders confirmed?

Once an order is confirmed, the operational workflow is triggered. The confirmed order becomes visible across all relevant applications (Admin backend, cutting app, delivery app). This step is crucial for maintaining operational efficiency and ensuring data accuracy.
