# Automation to quotes accepted by online acceptance, How does this work, next steps

**How****It** **Works**

Online Acceptance: When a quote is accepted(i.e., when the customer pays the quote) through the online platform, the system automatically receives and processes the acceptance notification.

**Next** **Steps:**

The acceptance triggers an automated workflow. This includes updating the status of the quote in the database, creating the Xero invoice, and sending an acceptance email to the customer.
