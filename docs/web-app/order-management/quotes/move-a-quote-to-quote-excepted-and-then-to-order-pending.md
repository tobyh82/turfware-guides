# Move a quote to quote excepted and then to order pending

- On the Quote management page, click the green “**Convert****To****Order**” button at the top. The confirmation box would pop up to confirm the Turf you want to convert to order. (See figure below)

- After clicking the “Convert” button, you will be navigated to the order management page. The order status is “Pending”.
