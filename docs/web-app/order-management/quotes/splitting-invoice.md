# Splitting Invoice

Follow these steps to make deposit and final invoice of an Order:

1. **Setup Xero Deposit Account**
   - Go to **System Settings** from the left sidebar menu.
   - Select **Company Information**.
   - In the **Deposit Payment Xero Account Code** section, select the appropriate Chart of Account for deposits.
2. **Create a Quote**
   - Go to Order Management from the left sidebar menu.
   - Select Quote.
   - Click the Create Quote button.
3. **Send the Quote**
   - Open the quote and click the **Send Quote** button.
   - Enter the **deposit percentage** the customer needs to pay.
   - Press **Send**.
4. **Customer Accept the Quote**
   - The customer will receive the quote via email.
   - Once they review the email, they can accept the quote by select the desired turf variety and click **Accept Quote**.
   - This action will:
     - Generate the **Deposit Invoice**.
     - Redirect the customer to the deposit payment page.
   - The customer completes the deposit payment.
5. **Send the final Invoice**
   - After the quote is accepted, an order is automatically created. Refer to [Handling Accepted Quote](../../../web-app/order-management/quotes-accepted/automation-to-quotes-accepted-by-online-acceptance-how-does-this-work-next-steps.md) for more details.
   - Open the order (make sure it is confirmed).
   - Click the **down arrow** next to the **Save** button and select **Send Final Invoice**.
   - The customer will receive the final invoice with the deposit amount already deducted.
