# Send a quote

- On the quote management page, click the green “Send Quote” buttonat the top.

- A preview will pop up that allows you to review or edit the quote email subject, content and receiver (See figure A below). Then click the green “**Send**” button at the bottom left.

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/22702/4350811/5.1_quotes_-_Attachment_1.jpg)  
Figure A

- A confirmation box will appear to confirm the receiver's email address before sending the email. (See figure B below)

![](https://static.helpjuice.com/helpjuice_production/uploads/upload/image/22702/4350812/5.1_quotes_-_Attachment_2.jpg)
