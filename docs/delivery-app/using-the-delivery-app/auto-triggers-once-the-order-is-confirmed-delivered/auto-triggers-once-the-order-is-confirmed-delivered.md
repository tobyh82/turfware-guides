# Auto triggers once the order is confirmed delivered

Once an order is confirmed as delivered:

- An email will be sent to the customer, including an image as verification of delivery, the customer’s signature (if they were present and signed), and a request for feedback.

- Another email will be sent to the customer of the next order to notify them of the estimated time of arrival (ETA) based on the current location.
