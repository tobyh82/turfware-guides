# Completing delivery

To complete the delivery:

- The driver should take or upload a photo as verification of delivery.

- Ask the customer to sign by clicking on the “Tap to sign” box under “Customer Signature.”

- If the customer was not present, tick the box “Customer was not present to sign.”

- Then click the “Mark as Delivered” button.

The completed delivery order will be moved to the completed orders section. To access it, click on the “Complete” option in the bottom navigation bar.
